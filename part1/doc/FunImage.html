Generic function images


Section Image.

Theorem image_eq_fun {U: Set}{V: Set} : ∀ (X: Ensemble U) (f g: U → V), (∀ x, f(x) = g(x)) → Im U V X f = Im U V X g.

  Theorem image_eq_set {U: Set}{V: Set}: ∀ (X X':Ensemble U) (f: U → V) , (∀ x: U, X x ↔ X' x) → Im U V X f = Im U V X' f.

  Theorem image_empty {U: Set}{V: Set} : ∀ X f, X = Empty_set U ↔ Im U V X f = Empty_set V.

  Theorem image_non_empty {U: Set}{V: Set} : ∀ X f, X ≠ Empty_set U ↔ Im U V X f ≠ Empty_set V.

  Theorem image_included {U: Set}{V: Set} : ∀ (X X': Ensemble U) (f: U → V), Included U X X' → Included V (Im U V X f) (Im U V X' f).

  Theorem image_union {U: Set}{V: Set} : ∀ (X X': Ensemble U) (f: U → V), Union V (Im U V X f) (Im U V X' f) = Im U V (Union U X X') f.

 End Image.
